About

Hi, I’m Matt.

I recently graduated from the University of Pittsburgh with a Master in Applied Statistics. I got to work on some exciting projects in computational biology, I’m currently working on some collaborations with others, and I’m looking to work on some more. Please drop me a line at matt dot broerman at gmail dott com. Résumé available upon request.

Here I’ll be blogging mostly with R and Python about my interests in climate, the justice system, and bayesian methods, with some tips, reflections, and art along the way.
